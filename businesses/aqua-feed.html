    Aqua Feed

    Aiming at providing complete solutions in Aquaculture; delivering enhanced and sustainable yields through innovative products and services

     

    In the last three decades, the Indian Aquaculture industry has evolved from being just a traditional practice to research-driven, commercial farming. We offer a wide range of specially designed Aqua Feed products to meet the nutritional requirements of fish and shrimps reared in Indian farming conditions. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing plants, equipped with quality assurance labs and research and

    development centre, help in developing nutritionally balanced and scientifically formulated products

    Our products help in weight gain and uniformity, increase survival rate and improve the feed conversion ratio. We also work closely with farmers and offer solutions for better pond management and higher farm profitability

     
     

    Shrimp Feed

    Shrimp Feed

    With the rapidly growing global demand for frozen shrimps, India has actively placed herself among the top producers and exporters of shrimp in the world

    More
     
     
     

    Nearly 70% of the total value of the seafood exports from India comes from frozen shrimps. The products in our Shrimp Feed portfolio have been developed to meet the requirements and demand of the organized shrimp industry.

    They are prepared with a balanced combination of nutrients under the guidance of experts at our advanced manufacturing units, to provide nutrition and boost the immunity of the crop.

     
     

    Fish Feed

    Fish Feed

    India is the second largest producer of fish in the world. With the growing demand for fresh water fish in the Indian market

    More
     
     
     

    the fish farming segment is expected to expand significantly. Our product portfolio is scientifically designed to improve farming practices (shifting from traditional farming to scientific farming),

    including the economics of the culture and delivering excellent growth. We offer floating and sinking feed for all stages and types of culture.

     
     

    Aqua Health Center

    Aqua Health Center

     

    Aquaculture has evolved into a sophisticated technology-based system for the culture of various aquatic species in controlled conditions

    More
     
     
     

    Aqua heath care products play a pivotal role in animal health management and the management of the culture environment. The main functions include soil and water management, gut health management, enhancement of natural aquatic productivity and eradication of pathogens to prevent the outbreak of diseases.

    Godrej Living Acua offers a complete solution with a diversified portfolio for the overall management of the crop.
