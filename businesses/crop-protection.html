    Crop Protection

    Improving farm productivity

     

    Our Crop Protection business has a wide range of products that cater to the entire crop lifecycle. Our constantly evolving product portfolio includes Plant Growth Regulators, Organic Manures, Generic Agrochemicals and Specialised Herbicides. Today, we are the world’s largest producers and marketers of Homobrassinolides. We are also the leader in selective post-emergence cotton herbicides in India

    We are constantly looking for ways to innovate and improve farm productivity at our Research & Development Centre, which is recognised by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India. We also collaborate with a number of multinational companies and Japanese research firms

     
     

    Plant Growth Regulators and Bio-Stimulants

    Plant Growth Regulators and Bio-Stimulants

    Our flagship product category is well known for exclusive in-house brands like Vipul, Double and Combine, market leaders in their respective categories in India

    More
     
     
     

    We are also the world’s largest producers and marketers in Homobrassinolides and export to the U.S.A., Kenya and Mauritius.

    All our products provide specific, scientific benefits to crops, thus improving yield with less input requirements.

     
     

    Herbicides

    Herbicides

    We are leaders in the post-emergence cotton herbicide segment in India

    More
     
     
     

    Our brand Hitweed, is India’s first selective cotton herbicide, developed through continuous research and extensive field trials. It provides a unique, easy to use solution to cotton farmers by selectively controlling broad-leaved weeds and preserving the health of the cotton crop.

    We are innovating on a ready pipeline of products to provide complete solutions for weed control in cotton, paddy, tea, sugarcane, corn and horticultural crops.

     
     

    Insecticides

    Insecticides

     

    Our extensive range of insecticides offers solutions for a wide range of crop-pest complexes

    More
     
     
     

    Our product portfolio includes molecules from every chemical group of insecticides, ranging from Organophosphates and Acaricides, to the recent Diamides.

    Our Research & Development team is innovating on effective farm protection solutions.

     
     

    Fungicides

    Fungicides

     

    We offer a wide range of fungicides for grapes, potato, paddy, cotton and some horticulture crops

    More
     
     
     

    Our products cater to fungal problems ranging from mildews and blights, to wilts and blasts. To eliminate the problem of identifying the correct

    fungal infection, we have successfully established brands of combination fungicides that have more than one mode of action.

     
     

    Soil Conditioners and Organic Manures

    Soil Conditioners and Organic Manures

     

    In addition to crop health, we also offer solutions for soil health with our range of Soil Conditioners and Organic Manures

    More
     
     
     

    The products are composed organically and serve as nutrients for farms, with no adverse impact on soil or plants.

    We use Neem as a primary ingredient in some of our variants, since it serves as a natural deterrent to several soil borne insects.

     
     
    About Godrej Agrovet

    Godrej Agrovet is a food and agri conglomerate, dedicated to improving the productivity of Indian farmers by innovating products and services that sustainably increase crop and livestock yields.
