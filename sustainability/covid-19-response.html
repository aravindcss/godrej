    Supporting
    local communities

     
     

    India

    Provided ration to over 10,685 contract workers at all Godrej Consumer Products and Godrej Agrovet sites, and to 8,900 construction workers at Godrej Properties sites

    Supported 30,169 families around Godrej Consumer Products and Godrej Agrovet sites with food and hygiene kits and area fumigation

     

    Tanzania

    Voluntary employee contribution to provide food supplies to blue collar workers

     

    Nepal

    Distributed 3,820 Godrej No. 1 soaps, 2,750 Goodknight Octa Coils and 820 Magic hand washes among communities, 44 hospitals, 47 police stations and various government offices

    Protecting people
    in our ecosystem

     
     

    India

    Provided safety kits for over 46,120 people, including contract workers, Godrej Consumer Products transporters, field sales personnel and Godrej Professional salonists

    Made direct cash transfers to 250 market research agents to help meet the basic needs of families

     

     

    Kenya, Tanzania

    Distributed sanitisers among 60 local consumers in Tanzania

    Supporting salons by providing 100,000 reusable masks, 20,000 litres of disinfectants, 10,000 litres of liquid hand wash, 3,000 face shields and conducting weekly disinfection drives

     

    Zambia, Ghana,
    Nigeria

    Voluntary employee contribution to support out of work hair stylists

    Voluntary employee contribution provided food packages for 100 widows, aged people and community and contract workers in Ghana

    Provided cash support of 1,000 INR per stylist for 500 stylists in Nigeria

    Donated 700 face masks to students and staff of a local primary school in Zambia

     

    Argentina, Chile, Uruguay

    In the first three months of the pandemic, we expanded our product portfolio in Argentina and Chile to include products like liquid soap, alcohol gel and antibacterial soap, ensuring that our communities have access to essential hygiene products

    Mandatory work from home measures

    Initiated work from home measures before government mandates

     

    ‘A day for myself’ initiative

    Introduced ‘A day for myself’ initiative, allowing Godrejites to plan a mandatory day off and spend it doing something they enjoy

     

    Protecting on-ground partners

    Extended COVID-19 medical insurance and added life insurance policies for our supply and distribution partners

     

    Prioritising mental wellbeing

    Amped up conversations around mental health, offering our team members to access to counselling services and self-help resources

     
     

    Strengthening
    public healthcare

     
     

    India

    Mumbai: Procured and delivered supplies worth INR 6.97 crore for the Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC). Supported 2 quarantine centers with a total capacity of 450 beds, set up a 75-bed isolation facility, provided 3 mobile testing autorickshaws to the BMC and oxygen support to urban settlements.

    Contributed 115 hospitals beds to the Government of Maharashtra

    Godrej Consumer Products provided 10,000 PPE kits in Mumbai and Delhi, supported local authorities around manufacturing sites for health equipment

     

    Mozambique

    Voluntary employee contribution used to manufacture masks and support local government efforts

    Donated 1 disinfection tunnel to the government

    Protecting school children in Kenya

    Provided 500 units of our Lavik hand wash, 5 units of Lavik disinfectant and over 1,000 reusable facemasks to children from the Mlolongo Primary School in Nairobi

     

    Protecting Indonesia

    Saniter partnered with Kereta Commuter Indonesia to provide 10,000 hand sanitiser gel pumps and 1 giant hand sanitiser spread across the commuter line

     

    Saniter collaborates with Gojek

    Saniter collaborated with Gojek to provide Saniter hand sanitiser, air and surface sanitisers and fabric sprays across 25 locations in 7 major Indonesian cities

     

    Supporting vulnerable communities

    Supported vulnerable communities in Latin America by donating 10,000 bottles of sanitisers to homes for children and adolescents, addiction centers and health centers

     

    Spreading awareness about hand hygiene

    Launched the #ProtektIndiaMovement in partnership with Godrej protekt to spread awareness around the importance of hand hygiene

     
     

    Providing relief to the
    most affected populations

     
     

    India

    Provided food relief and/or hygiene kits to over 43,874 families across India

    Supplied 96,000 meals from our global headquarters Godrej One; supported in providing 25,000 cooked meals in Mumbai

     

    Argentina

    Distributed 10,000 sanitisers and liquid hand washes to NGOs

     

    USA

    Distributed 1,50,000 sanitisers to 100 charities

     

    Indonesia

    Distributed food kits to impacted families, and nursing and disability homes

     

     

    Argentina, Chile, Uruguay

    Voluntary employee contribution to provide food and clothes to homeless people.

    Boosting youth employment in Ghana

    Partnered the United Nations Habitat to offer employment to over 450 youth from local slums at our new manufacturing facility in Tema, Acra

     

    Supporting salons in Nigeria

    Launched a relief fund to help with the purchase of essentials for members of the small scale hairstyling community in Nigeria

     

    Reviving salon businesses in Ghana

    Spearheaded a "Back to Life" campaign, donated to the Hairdresser Association, carried out salon hygiene activities to ensure safety, partnered the Ministry of Health and distributed start-up kits to help salons kickstart their business

     

    Suraksha Salon programme

    Donated safety kits and back to business digital kits, and provided education on safety and hygiene measures, salon sanitisation, etc. to 10,000 salons in India as part of Godrej Professional’s Suraksha Salon programme

     

    Helping salons in Latin America

    Distributed safety kits with chinstraps, masks, reusable layers and sanitisers, provided free webinars on ways to leverage social media, tips to improve profits and diversify business, among Issue Hair Colour’s salon partners in Latin America

    government efforts

    Extended monetary and/or protective supplies to state and local administrations

    Godrej Group companies matched the voluntary payroll contribution by over 4,400 Godrejites and donated INR 6.98 crore to the PM CARES Fund

     

    South Africa

    Donated face masks to the government

     

    UAE

    Voluntary employee contribution directed to battling national COVID-19 efforts

     

    Uganda

    Donated INR 40 lakh towards government efforts

     

    Indonesia

    Voluntary employee contribution of INR 10 lakh

    Donated our products to local communities and 74 hospitals

    Donated face masks and PPEs to frontline workers

     

    Kenya

    Voluntary employee contribution to support local communities and government efforts
