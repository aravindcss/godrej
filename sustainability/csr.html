    Our approach

    The effect of climate change on the agriculture sector is adverse. Our integrated CSR programme focuses on women empowerment, economic growth, water management and mitigating farmers' vulnerability to climate change impact. We work with landless and marginal farmers, especially women, in arid and semi-arid regions.
